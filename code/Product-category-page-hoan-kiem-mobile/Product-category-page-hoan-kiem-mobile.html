A Legacy Written in Stone

Hoan Kiem is not just a district; it is the living heart of Hanoi. Here, the legendary "Sword Lake" serves as a serene mirror to the city's evolution. From the bustling commerce of the 36 Streets to the grandeur of the French Quarter, every corner whispers stories of dynasties and revolutions.

Our collection in Hoan Kiem prioritizes high-discerning travelers who seek more than a stay—they seek a connection to the soul of the capital.
